What We Provide

For People

Connect people with charities that answer what they need efficiently & allow people to rate the charities according to what they provide which helps reveal the truth about how each charity work.

For Charities

Connect charities with more people to help, exposing them to more donors, unifying their data to avoid repition of accounts, and providing a space to put their data and do their work efficiently.

For Donors

Connect donors with charities through providing a database about charities that are rated by people which allows them choose most efficient charity.
